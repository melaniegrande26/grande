Local Cat Convinced It Has Never Been Fed

By: A. Scratchpost | Staff Reporter

A Tragic Tale of an Empty Bowl

In a shocking turn of events, local resident Mr. Whiskers, a 4-year-old Tabby, claims he has not been fed in "approximately one thousand years." This statement comes despite eyewitness accounts confirming he finished a bowl of premium tuna-flavored kibble exactly twelve minutes ago.

Mr. Whiskers, speaking through a series of increasingly loud and dramatic meows, demonstrated the severity of his condition by showing his owners the very bottom of the ceramic bowl, which was clearly visible through a small gap in the biscuits.

"The situation is dire," his owner, Sarah, reported. "He has already collapsed twice onto my laptop keyboard while I was trying to work. He's currently looking at the refrigerator as if it contains the only hope for his survival."

Evidence of the 'Great Hunger'

  • Intense staring at the cupboard door.
  • Aggressive weaving between the legs of anyone walking toward the kitchen.
  • A complete refusal to acknowledge the half-full bowl of water next to him.

At the time of reporting, Mr. Whiskers has decided to take a nap in a sunbeam, though experts suggest he will resume his protest the moment a can opener is heard anywhere in the tri-state area.
